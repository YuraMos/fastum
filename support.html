Quick answers

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Open the App Store app, tap your profile, then Subscriptions, and choose Fastum. You can change or cancel there. Note: deleting the app does not cancel a subscription.

Where is my fasting data stored?

On your device. Fastum doesn’t keep your fasting data on any server. To remove it, use Clear history in the app or delete the app.

My notifications aren’t showing up.

Make sure notifications are allowed for Fastum in your device Settings → Notifications → Fastum, and that Focus/Do Not Disturb isn’t silencing them.

Does my fast keep counting if I close the app?

Yes. Fastum tracks your fast from the time you started, so closing or backgrounding the app doesn’t lose any time, and your stage reminders still arrive.

Is Fastum medical advice?

No. Fastum is a wellness tool, not medical advice. Please consult a doctor before fasting, especially for longer fasts. See our Terms.
