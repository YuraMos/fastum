Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 6, 2026 · Last updated: July 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Fastum (“the App”, “we”, “us”, “our”) handles your information. Fastum is provided by Yurii Moskviak. By creating an account or using Fastum, you acknowledge the practices described here.

In short: Fastum gives you an account so your fasting history and profile sync across your devices and survive reinstalling the App. To do this we collect your email and name (from Sign in with Apple or email sign-up), your profile (goal, experience, diet, optional nickname and photo), and your fasting history. We store this with our backend provider. We do not sell your data, show ads, or use third-party tracking — and you can delete your account and all of its data at any time from inside the App.

1. Scope

This policy applies to the Fastum mobile application. It does not apply to Apple’s App Store or to any third-party services you may separately use.

2. Information we collect

Account & identity. To create your account we collect your email address and, where provided, your name. If you use Sign in with Apple, Apple gives us a unique identifier and, only if you allow it, your name and email address (which may be a private Apple “relay” address). If you sign up with email and password, we store your email and a securely hashed version of your password (we never store your actual password).

Profile. The information you provide in onboarding and on your profile: your fasting goal, experience level, diet preference, optional nickname, unit preference, and — if you choose to add one — a profile photo.

Fasting data. The fasts you record: start and end times, chosen lengths, whether goals were reached, and your history over time.

Notifications. Fasting-stage reminders are scheduled and delivered locally on your device by the operating system. We do not operate a push-notification server and do not collect a device push token.

Diagnostics. If you have enabled sharing analytics with developers in your device settings, Apple may give us aggregated, anonymized usage and crash statistics through App Store Connect. This does not identify you and can be turned off in your device settings.

No advertising or third-party tracking. Fastum contains no third-party advertising and no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. We do not track you across other apps or websites.

3. How we use your information

We use your information only to: (a) create and secure your account and sign you in; (b) store and sync your profile and fasting history across your devices so it is not lost; (c) run your fasting timer and local reminders; and (d) maintain, troubleshoot, secure, and improve the App. We do not use your information for advertising, and we do not sell it.

4. Where your data is stored

Your account, profile, photo, and fasting history are stored with Convex, Inc., our backend and database provider, which hosts and processes this data on our behalf (as our service provider) on infrastructure located in the United States. Data is transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS/TLS) connections. Convex’s privacy policy: convex.dev/privacy.

5. Legal bases (EEA / UK users)

Where the EU or UK GDPR applies, we rely on: performance of a contract (to provide your account and the App); our legitimate interests (to keep the App working and secure); and, where applicable, your consent (which you may withdraw at any time).

6. How information is shared

We do not sell or rent your information. It is shared only with: Apple (for Sign in with Apple and App Store services); Convex (hosting and storage, as our processor, described above); for legal and safety reasons if required by law or to protect our rights, our users, or the public; and in a business transfer, in which case this policy continues to apply or you will be notified of any new policy.

7. No sale or “sharing” for advertising

We do not sell your personal information and do not “share” it for cross-context behavioral advertising, as those terms are defined under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA) or similar laws.

8. Data retention and deletion

We keep your account information, profile, and fasting history for as long as your account exists. You can delete your account at any time using Delete Account in the App’s Profile screen — this permanently removes your profile, photo, fasting history, and account from our systems. You can also delete individual data (for example, clear your history) within the App.

9. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including transmitting it over encrypted connections and storing passwords only as secure hashes. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, but we work to protect your data. You are responsible for keeping your sign-in credentials and device secure.

10. International transfers

Your data is processed in the United States by our providers, which may have different data-protection laws than your own country. Where required, appropriate safeguards are applied by those providers.

11. Your privacy rights

You can access and edit your profile in the App and delete your account and data at any time via Delete Account. Depending on where you live (for example, the EEA, the UK, or California), you may also have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your personal information, to object to or restrict certain processing, and to not be discriminated against for exercising these rights. To make a request, contact us using the details below; we will respond as required by applicable law.

12. “Do Not Track”

The App does not track you across other apps or websites, so it does not respond to browser “Do Not Track” signals.

13. Children

Fastum is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

14. Purchases

Fastum does not currently offer in-app purchases. If we add subscriptions in the future, payments will be processed by Apple through your App Store account — we would never receive your payment-card details — and we will update this policy before doing so.

15. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will revise the “Last updated” date, and material changes will be highlighted in the App or on this page. Your continued use after an update indicates your acknowledgment of the revised policy.

16. Contact

If you have questions or requests about this policy or your information, contact us at support@yuramoskviak.com.
