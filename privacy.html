Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 6, 2026 · Last updated: July 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Fastum (“the App”, “we”, “us”, “our”) handles information in connection with your use of the App. Fastum is provided by Yurii Moskviak. By downloading or using Fastum, you acknowledge the practices described here.

In short: Fastum stores your fasting data on your device. We do not operate our own servers, we do not collect your name or email address, we do not use advertising or third-party tracking, and we do not sell your information.

1. Scope

This policy applies to the Fastum mobile application. It does not apply to Apple’s App Store, or to any third-party services you may separately use.

2. Information we handle

Fasting data you create. Your fasts (start times, chosen lengths, history) and your diet preference are stored locally on your device using standard operating-system storage. This data is not transmitted to us and remains on your device until you clear it in the App or delete the App.

Notifications. Fasting reminders are scheduled and delivered locally on your device by the operating system. We do not operate a push-notification server.

Subscription and purchase data. If you buy Fastum Pro, Apple processes the payment through your App Store account; we never receive your payment card or billing address. To verify and restore subscriptions we use RevenueCat, Inc., which receives a randomly generated app-user identifier, purchase and transaction details, and basic device and operating-system information. RevenueCat does not receive your name or email. RevenueCat’s privacy policy: revenuecat.com/privacy.

Diagnostics. If you have enabled sharing of analytics with developers in your device settings, Apple may provide us with aggregated, anonymized usage and crash statistics through App Store Connect. This data does not identify you, and you can turn it off in your device’s privacy settings.

No advertising or third-party tracking. Fastum does not contain third-party advertising and does not include third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. If this ever changes, we will update this policy and obtain consent where required.

3. How we use information

We use the limited information above only to: (a) run your fasting timer and local notifications on your device; (b) verify, restore, and manage your Fastum Pro subscription; and (c) maintain, secure, and improve the App.

4. Legal bases (EEA / UK users)

Where the EU or UK GDPR applies, we rely on: performance of a contract (to provide the App and your subscription); our legitimate interests (to keep the App working and secure); and, where applicable, your consent (which you may withdraw at any time).

5. How information is shared

We do not sell or rent your information. Information is shared only with Apple (payment processing and App Store services); RevenueCat (subscription management, as described above); for legal and safety reasons if required by law or to protect our rights, users, or the public; and in a business transfer, in which case this policy continues to apply or you will be notified of any new policy.

6. No sale or “sharing” of personal information

We do not sell your personal information and do not “share” it for cross-context behavioral advertising, as those terms are defined under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA) or similar laws.

7. Data retention

Your fasting data is retained on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App. Subscription records held by Apple and RevenueCat are retained under their own policies.

8. Security

We design the App to keep your data on your device. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect the limited information involved. You are responsible for securing access to your own device.

9. International transfers

Apple and RevenueCat may process subscription-related data in the United States and other countries, which may have different data-protection laws than your own. Where required, appropriate safeguards are applied by those providers.

10. Your privacy rights

Because your fasting data lives on your device, you can access, edit, or delete it directly in the App or by uninstalling. Depending on where you live (for example, the EEA, the UK, or California), you may also have rights to access, correct, delete, or port personal information, to object to or restrict certain processing, and to not be discriminated against for exercising these rights. To make a request regarding subscription data, contact us using the details below; you may also contact Apple or RevenueCat directly. We will respond as required by applicable law.

11. “Do Not Track”

The App does not track you across other apps or websites, so it does not respond to browser “Do Not Track” signals.

12. Children

Fastum is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will address it.

13. Third-party services and links

The App relies on Apple and RevenueCat as described above. Their handling of information is governed by their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

14. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will revise the “Last updated” date, and material changes will be highlighted in the App or on this page. Your continued use after an update indicates your acknowledgment of the revised policy.

15. Contact

If you have questions or requests about this policy or your information, contact us at support@yuramoskviak.com.
